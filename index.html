From the Founder

Healthcare That Comes to You

Dhwaj Medical Services was born from a deeply personal understanding of how difficult it can be for patients — the elderly, the post-surgical, the disabled — to access quality diagnostics. Too often, the journey to a hospital itself becomes an obstacle to care.

Our mission is simple: remove that barrier entirely. We bring the clinic to your doorstep — with the same medical-grade equipment, the same qualified professionals, and the same quality you would expect at any leading diagnostic centre.

We don't just provide medical services — we provide peace of mind. When a family calls us at midnight for a home X-Ray or an ECG, they are placing their trust in us completely. That responsibility drives everything we do.

— Founder, Dhwaj Medical Services

Based in Kandivali (W), we have proudly served hundreds of patients across Mumbai's western suburbs, and we remain committed to making doorstep healthcare the standard, not the exception.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your home medical visit.

We cover Kandivali, Borivali, Malad, Goregaon, Andheri, Dahisar, Mira Road, Jogeshwari, Charkop, Lokhandwala and all nearby localities in Mumbai's western suburbs. If you're unsure whether we service your area, just call or WhatsApp us and we'll confirm immediately.

For most services, we can typically arrange a visit within 2–4 hours of booking. For emergencies like ambulance services, our response time is much faster. We operate 24/7, so even late-night and early-morning calls are welcomed.

Yes. We use medical-grade, calibrated digital X-Ray machines operated by certified radiographers. The quality of imaging meets clinical standards and the reports are equivalent to those you would receive at a diagnostic centre. Reports are reviewed and delivered digitally.

Reports are shared via WhatsApp or email, typically within a few hours for ECG and same/next day for pathology, depending on the tests ordered. Hard copy reports can also be arranged on request.

Absolutely. Every team member — radiographer, ECG technician, phlebotomist, and paramedic — is formally trained, certified, and background verified. Patient safety and dignity are our absolute priority on every visit.

Our pricing is transparent and affordable, with no hidden charges. Rates vary by service type and specific tests required. Please call or WhatsApp us for an instant quote — we will provide a clear, all-inclusive price before any commitment.

Yes. Our ambulance service covers both emergencies and planned transfers — including inter-hospital transport, discharge from hospital to home, and transport for patients needing routine outpatient procedures. Our trained paramedics accompany all transfers for patient safety.

Yes, where clinically appropriate, we can combine services — for example, an X-Ray and ECG in a single home visit. Let us know your requirements when you book and we'll arrange the right team and equipment for a combined visit.
